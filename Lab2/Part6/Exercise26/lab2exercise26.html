To interpolate a date interpolateDate() can be used.

InterpolatDate() takes in 2 dates dates and produces a new date value based on the weighting given.

InterpolateDate takes in two dates using new Date() and produces a new Date() object. With dateIntr(0.5), a new date is produced directly half way inbetween the two dates
